# Demo Script

## 2-Minute Demo Pitch

AI Social Copilot helps people who struggle with casual workplace communication feel more confident and included. The user selects a workplace scenario like a team meeting, lunch break, or hallway chat, adds a little context, and gets tailored conversation starters, follow-up replies, cultural communication guidance, and a mini practice simulation. Instead of feeling stuck or isolated, they get support in the exact moments that shape belonging, trust, and collaboration at work.

## Demo Flow

1. Open the app homepage and introduce the problem.
2. Select a role such as international student or new intern.
3. Choose a scenario such as lunch break or team meeting.
4. Select a goal like starting a conversation or keeping it going.
5. Add context about feeling awkward, new, or unsure what to say.
6. Click generate and point out that the app is calling a live AI backend, not just using static copy.
7. Show the conversation starters, follow-up responses, what-to-say-next guidance, and cultural tips.
8. Scroll to the practice simulation and explain how this helps users rehearse in a low-pressure way.
9. Close with the future vision: voice coaching, multilingual support, and personalized workplace guidance.

## What To Say During The Demo

This is not just a chatbot. It is a confidence-building copilot for real workplace moments that are socially small but emotionally high-stakes. The goal is to help people feel included, communicate better, and build relationships faster in unfamiliar professional settings.

## Suggested Talk Track

Hi, this is AI Social Copilot. We built it for international students, interns, and early-career professionals who often know what they want to say at work, but freeze in socially awkward moments like lunch breaks, hallway chats, or post-meeting conversations.

Here, the user picks their role, the scenario, the communication goal, and their preferred tone, then adds a little context. When I click generate, the app sends that situation to a live AI backend and returns structured workplace guidance in real time.

Instead of one generic answer, the user gets several conversation starters, follow-up responses, suggestions for what to say next, culturally aware communication tips, and even a short practice dialogue to rehearse with. That makes the product useful not only in the moment, but also as a confidence-building tool over time.

The long-term vision is to expand this into voice-based coaching, multilingual support, personalized onboarding, and organization-specific workplace guidance. So this is both a practical product for today and a scalable platform for inclusion and communication support in the future.
