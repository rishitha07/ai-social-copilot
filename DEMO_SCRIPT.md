# Demo Script

## 60-Second Pitch

AI Social Copilot helps people who struggle with casual workplace communication feel more confident and included. The user selects a workplace scenario like a team meeting, lunch break, or hallway chat, adds a little context, and instantly gets tailored conversation starters, follow-up replies, and cultural communication guidance. Instead of feeling stuck or isolated, they get support in the exact moments that shape belonging and collaboration at work.

## Demo Flow

1. Open the app homepage and introduce the problem.
2. Select a role such as international student or new intern.
3. Choose a scenario such as lunch break or team meeting.
4. Select a goal like starting a conversation or keeping it going.
5. Add context about feeling awkward, new, or unsure what to say.
6. Click generate and show the conversation starters, follow-up responses, and tips.
7. Scroll to the practice simulation and explain how this helps users rehearse in a low-pressure way.
8. Close with the future vision: real LLM integration, voice coaching, multilingual support, and personalized workplace guidance.

## What To Say During The Demo

This is not just a chatbot. It is a confidence-building copilot for real workplace moments that are socially small but emotionally high-stakes. The goal is to help people feel included, communicate better, and build relationships faster in unfamiliar professional settings.
